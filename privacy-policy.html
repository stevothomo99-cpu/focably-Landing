Focably is committed to protecting the privacy of children and families. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights — in plain English.

Effective date: 16 June 2025  |  Jurisdiction: Australia & New Zealand

1. Who We Are

Focably is operated by Your Finance Dept Pty Ltd (ABN to be inserted), trading as Focably, based in Queensland, Australia.

We operate the Focably web application (focably.vercel.app) and the FocablyED landing page (focablyed.com). References to "Focably", "we", "us" or "our" in this policy refer to Your Finance Dept Pty Ltd.

For privacy enquiries contact us at: privacy@focablyed.com

2. Who This Policy Applies To

This policy applies to all users of Focably including:

  • Students (primary and high school aged children)
  • Parents and guardians who create family accounts
  • Teachers who connect to student accounts
  • Visitors to our landing page and waitlist

⚠️ Children's data: Focably is designed for use by children under parental supervision. Students under 18 must have a parent or guardian create and manage their family account. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without verified parental consent via our family linking system.

3. What Information We Collect

Account information

  • Email address (parent, teacher, or student)
  • First name
  • Role (student, parent, or teacher)
  • Age group (primary or high school) — for students
  • Avatar selection (emoji or optional photo)
  • School name (optional, for teacher accounts)

App usage data

  • Tasks created, completed, and their due dates
  • Assignment titles and descriptions
  • Proof of task completion (text descriptions submitted by students)
  • XP points and streak data
  • Trust score history
  • Theme and display preferences

Subscription and billing

  • Subscription status (free or Pro)
  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe — we do not store credit card numbers or payment details
  • Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data: stripe.com/au/privacy

Technical data

  • Push notification subscription tokens (if you enable notifications)
  • Browser type and device type (basic, not fingerprinted)
  • Page visit data via Vercel hosting (aggregate, not personal)

Waitlist data (focablyed.com)

  • Name, email, mobile number, and role interest submitted via the waitlist form
  • This data is stored in our database and may be synced to HubSpot CRM for follow-up communications

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the Focably app and its features
  • To connect students, parents, and teachers within a family or class
  • To send push notifications you have opted into
  • To process Pro subscription payments via Stripe
  • To respond to support requests
  • To contact waitlist members about Focably updates and launch (with the ability to unsubscribe at any time)
  • To improve the app based on usage patterns (aggregate, not individual profiling)

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not sell your data to third parties. Ever.

5. Who Can See Your Data

Within the app

  • Parents can see their linked child's tasks, proof submissions, XP, and trust score
  • Teachers can only see tasks that have been explicitly shared with their class — private tasks (marked 🔒) are never visible to teachers
  • Students can see their own data only
  • No user can see another family's data

Third-party services we use

  • Supabase — database and authentication, hosted in Sydney, Australia (AWS ap-southeast-2). Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Stripe — payment processing. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — AI-powered assignment breakdown. Assignment text is sent to Anthropic's API for processing. Anthropic does not train on API data. Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — web hosting. Vercel Privacy Policy
  • HubSpot — waitlist CRM (waitlist data only, not app user data). HubSpot Privacy Policy

6. Data Storage and Security

All Focably user data is stored in Supabase, hosted in Sydney, Australia (AWS ap-southeast-2 region). Your data does not leave Australia except where third-party services listed above are involved.

We implement the following security measures:

  • All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • All data encrypted at rest in Supabase
  • Row-level security policies — users can only access their own data
  • Authentication handled by Supabase Auth (industry standard)
  • Payment data never touches our servers — processed directly by Stripe

7. Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and all associated data (see below)
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Complaints — lodge a complaint with the relevant authority (see Section 11)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@focablyed.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time:

  • In-app: Settings → Delete Account → Confirm
  • By email: privacy@focablyed.com with subject "Account Deletion Request"

When you delete your account we permanently remove:

  • Your profile and all personal information
  • All tasks, assignments, and proof submissions
  • XP, streak, and trust score history
  • Family and class connections
  • Push notification subscriptions

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Stripe subscription data is retained by Stripe per their own data retention policy.

For parent accounts: deleting your parent account will also remove your family record. Linked child accounts will be disconnected but not automatically deleted — you must delete each child's account separately or contact us to delete all family data at once.

9. Data Retention

  • Active account data is retained for as long as your account is open
  • Deleted account data is purged within 30 days of deletion request
  • Waitlist data is retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Stripe retains billing records per their own policy (typically 7 years for financial records)

10. Children's Privacy

Focably is designed for children aged 8 and above under parental supervision. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • Child accounts must be linked to a parent account before full access is granted
  • Parents control what tasks are visible to teachers
  • Private tasks are never visible to teachers at the database level — not just the UI
  • We do not serve advertising to any user, including children
  • We do not build advertising profiles on children
  • We do not share children's data with third parties except as listed in Section 5
  • AI features (Smart Import) are available to students — assignment text is sent to Anthropic's API. Anthropic does not train models on API data

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information without parental consent, contact us immediately at privacy@focablyed.com and we will delete it promptly.

12. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that is likely to cause serious harm:

  • Australia: We will notify affected individuals and the OAIC as soon as practicable and within 30 days of becoming aware
  • New Zealand: We will notify affected individuals and the NZ Privacy Commissioner within 72 hours of becoming aware

Notifications will include the nature of the breach, the information affected, and steps you can take to protect yourself.
